About Dollar Fence

At Dollar Fence, we're America's leading fence installation company with over 15 years of experience serving residential and commercial customers nationwide. Our team of certified professionals is committed to delivering exceptional quality and outstanding customer service on every project.

We specialize in all types of fencing including vinyl, wood, aluminum, chain link, steel, pool safety, and commercial security fencing. Every installation comes with our industry-leading warranty and satisfaction guarantee.

Nationwide Service Coverage

Dollar Fence proudly serves customers across the United States with local teams in over 100 cities. Whether you're located in California, Texas, Florida, New York, or anywhere in between, our experienced installers are ready to help with your fencing needs.

Our nationwide network ensures consistent quality standards, competitive pricing, and reliable service no matter where your property is located.
