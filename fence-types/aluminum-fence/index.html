Why Choose an Aluminum Fence?

Timeless Beauty

Aluminum fences bring elegance and structure to your property with a stately appearance that stands the test of time. Thanks to advanced coatings and finishes, they resist fading and chalking—even under intense sunlight and severe weather.

Extensive Variety

Choose from a wide range of styles, heights, grades, and decorative options to match your home's design. Whether you prefer industrial-grade strength or ornamental sophistication, Dollar Fence has the right fit.

Versatile for Any Landscape

Aluminum fences can be installed on flat or sloped terrain without leaving gaps—making them ideal for yards with grade changes or uneven surfaces. This feature also helps keep pets safely enclosed and unwanted wildlife out.

Low Maintenance

Aluminum fencing requires no painting, sealing, or staining. It doesn't rust, rot, warp, or attract pests—unlike wood, vinyl, or wrought iron. Occasional rinsing or clearing around the base is all that's needed to maintain its appearance.

Enhanced Security

While not designed for privacy, aluminum fences offer excellent perimeter security and can include spear-top pickets for added deterrence. They're perfect for pool enclosures, gated entries, and front yards where visibility and safety are both priorities.

Built for Strength

Aluminum holds up incredibly well in harsh climates and high-wind areas, making it an ideal option for hurricane-prone zones. It's stronger than wood or vinyl and resists bending or damage in extreme conditions.

Long-Term Value

Aluminum fencing gives you the elegant look of wrought iron at a fraction of the cost. It's lightweight, easier to install, and delivers excellent long-term value. While the upfront cost may be higher than wood, aluminum fencing pays for itself over time with zero maintenance and unmatched durability.
